Types of Visualizations

These are the various types of visualizations that you can generate based on the uploaded data
Scroll down and upload the CSV file, to get the Vizualization

GPA Histogram

A histogram displays the distribution of GPA scores, helping you understand the frequency of different GPA ranges.

GPA Bar Chart

A bar chart provides a visual comparison of GPA scores for different categories, such as courses or semesters.

GPA Pie Chart

A pie chart showcases the proportion of each GPA category in the dataset, aiding in visualizing percentage composition.

GPA Doughnut Chart

A doughnut chart is similar to a pie chart but with a hole in the center, offering an alternative view of the data distribution.

GPA Scatter Chart

A scatter chart plots individual GPA data points to show relationships or patterns between two variables.

Rank Table

A rank table lists the ranked performance of students based on their GPA scores.
